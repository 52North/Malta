About Malta | Extreme Events Viewer

Malta is a lightweight Javascript client to access a database of extreme events. It is open source software from 52°North's Geostatistics Community.

Development of Malta was supported by Deutsche Wetterdienst (DWD) Regional Climate Centre on Climate Monitoring (Europe and Middle East).
